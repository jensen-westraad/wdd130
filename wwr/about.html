About ~ White Water Rafting

Purpose

Our purpose is to create unforgettable memories through our adventurious experiences by connecting people with the power and beauty of South Africas wild rivers — safely, sustainably, and with a spirit of fun and exploration.

History

Founded in 2005, White Water Rafting was born from a deep passion for South Africas untamed rivers and a desire to share the thrill of white water rafting with adventurers from around the world. It all started when a small group of river guides, nature lovers, and outdoor educators saw an opportunity to turn their love for rafting into a business that celebrates both adrenaline and the environment.

Our first tours launched in 2006 on the Orange River, offering multi-day rafting trips through the breathtaking canyons of the Northern Cape. With only a handful of rafts, some camping gear, and a company truck, we began guiding small groups down the river, emphasizing personal connection, safety, and authentic South African hospitality for nearly 20 years.
