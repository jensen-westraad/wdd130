Adventure Awaits You!

Beginners Bank:

If you are just looking for a easy trial to kick off your White Water Rafting journey or just want a more calm approach for the kiddies, this is the path for you! Our shortest course that we offer in a calm section of the Tegula

The Plunge:

For more experienced rafters, this is a path for the risk takers and the daring. With the water getting faster and the rapids getting less forgiving. You are sure to come back drenched with water and pure adrenaline rush! Our longest course down the Orange River, providing a proper endurance test, only for the bold!

Crocodile Gorge:

Although you wont actually find any crocodiles on this path, you will wish that they were the least of your problem. Even for the Experts, you are signing up for a rude awakening and a full-body workout! Down Doring River, by far our most challenging course, we recommend you have some prior experience before attempting!
